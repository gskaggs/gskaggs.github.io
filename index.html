About me

I am a Turing Scholar studying Computer Science and Mathematics at the University of Texas at Austin (Class of 2022). Before that, I grew up in Houston, Texas just a few minutes away from the Johnson Space Center. I graduated Valedictorian from Clear Lake High School (Class of 2018).

You can find me on my blog, GitHub, and LinkedIn. You can also email me.

Work Experience

Duolingo

I'm working this summer as a software engineering intern for the language-learning company Duolingo.

Undergraduate Research Assistant

The summer following my freshman year I did research at the intersection of Machine Learning and Computer Architecture under the advisement of Dr. Calvin Lin. In particular, I applied recurrent neural networks to the problem of cache data prefetching, developing a two-layer LSTM neural model using TensorFlow and Python to predict prefetched addresses.

Mentorship

UT Computer Science Pods

UT Computer Science Pods are small groups of first-year students who take classes together and attend a weekly seminar led by their designated upperclassman Pod mentor. As a junior at UT Austin, I will serve as one such Pod mentor, helping students transition to life at UT and mentoring students on everything from career fair to undergraduate research.

Austin Chinese Educational Services

During my freshman summer I worked part-time as a Python and Scratch instructor for elementary and middle school students. For these two months I designed the course objectives and curriculum, taught daily lectures, and helped students with their course projects.
